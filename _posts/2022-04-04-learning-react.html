What I was doing recently? I must say a lot. I reviewed my JavaScript knowledge, looked into new things of ES6, TypeScript, React, etc.

I am changing my mind about React. As I mentioned before I used Vue for EduCards.

2 years ago when I had little idea of modern JavaScript and it's frameworks, React looked hard to me and writing SPA with it was close to impossible (mind I had no one to talk about principles and best(or even any) practices). Vue looked easier, more opinionated if you will.
They had their official router, state manager and great documentation. And with the help of tutorial and couple online courses EduCards was ready for production in less than 2 months.

This time I decided to look into React again, and you know it's not that difficult as it used to be 2 years ago. As I was said, I need to learn one framework and another one will be easier to pick up.
